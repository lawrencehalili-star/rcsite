Student Portal

The Richmindale College Student Portal is your one-stop resource center designed to streamline your academic experience. Through the portal, students can easily manage their academic journey by enrolling in courses, accessing orientation materials and guides, exploring a comprehensive library of academic resources, and downloading unofficial transcripts. The portal also allows students to view their student ledger and billing history, generate financial receipts, update their contact information, and stay connected with their Academic Advisor. It serves as an essential tool to stay informed, manage schedules, and track progress, all in one convenient location.

Upon enrollment, every student is assigned an Academic Advisor, who serves as their primary point of contact for academic and administrative support. Advisors assist with course selection, degree-completion plans, and academic progress, ensuring that students stay on track throughout their academic journey.
