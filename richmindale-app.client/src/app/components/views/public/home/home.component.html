Participate in Classes

Richmindale College classes are asynchronous, except for some activities that require student identity verification where students need to be present in an online meeting with a Richmindale representative.

Enrolled students are required to participate in classes using Richmindale's online learning management system (LMS) where students perform substantive activities.
